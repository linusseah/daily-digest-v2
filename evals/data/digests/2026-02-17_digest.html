Nathan Baschez shares a clever technique for reducing "cognitive debt" in AI-accelerated development: ask the LLM to write two versions of implementation plans—one technical, one as a narrative or even a webcomic. This helps maintain deep understanding when AI is generating code faster than you can fully comprehend it. Relevant as you build more agentic systems.
