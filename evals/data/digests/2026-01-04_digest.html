🧱 Strategic Consolidation & The AI Stack

ggml.ai Joins Hugging Face to Secure Local AI's Future
Georgi Gerganov (creator of llama.cpp) and the entire ggml.ai team joined Hugging Face. This consolidates the open-source inference stack: model hosting + distribution (HF) + local inference (llama.cpp) under one roof. Simon notes it's hard to overstate Gerganov's impact—llama.cpp made local LLMs viable back in March 2023, and the GGUF format has become the standard. The team maintains full autonomy over the project while HF provides sustainability.
OpenClaw Creator Joins OpenAI
Peter Steinberger, creator of OpenClaw (the viral computer-using agent with 200K+ stars), joined OpenAI in February. Notably, this wasn't an acquisition—despite multiple billion-dollar offers—and OpenClaw is transitioning to an independent foundation supported by OpenAI. Steinberger chose advancing personal AI agents for everyone over a corporate exit.
